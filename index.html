Frequently Asked Questions

What courses does Athenaeum offer? expand_more

Athenaeum Academy offers comprehensive online preparation for O Levels, A Levels, Matric Science/Arts, Intermediate (FSc), MDCAT, and ECAT. We also offer specialized tech skill development courses for all ages.

How much do courses cost? expand_more

Our courses are designed to be affordable while maintaining university-grade excellence. Specific pricing varies by program (O/A Levels, Board Exams, or Entry Tests). You can start with a 3-day free trial to experience our interactive classes before committing.

Can parents track their child's progress? expand_more

Yes! We provide a dedicated Parent Dashboard where you can monitor your child's attendance, weekly quiz scores, assignment submissions, and overall academic progress in real-time. We also offer a 24/7 parent helpline.
